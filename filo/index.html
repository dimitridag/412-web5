Mission

FILO, named after the Greek word for 'friend', is on a mission to combat loneliness among men through a user-friendly app. This app connects men based on shared interests and proximity, facilitating the formation of genuine friendships and participation in local social groups and events. FILO aims to reduce the isolation prevalent in modern society by fostering authentic connections that can improve mental well-being and overall happiness. We prioritize user safety and plan to expand our services to provide resources for men's mental health and personal growth, all while redefining how we address the issue of male loneliness in our communities.
