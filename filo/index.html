Filo for Friend

Mission

At FILO, which translates to 'friend' in Greek, we're committed to addressing loneliness among men. Our mission is to develop a user-friendly app that assists men in forming friendships and joining social groups based on their interests and location. We aim to cultivate authentic connections and combat the issue of isolation in contemporary society.
