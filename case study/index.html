Process

Game Conception

I envisioned an exciting endless scroller game set on a river, where players guide a frog character through the journey of dodging lily pads and capturing flies to score points. The game introduced a three-heart mechanism and a time limit, challenging players to navigate with precision and speed.

The ultimate goal is to earn as many points as possible while striving to survive as long as possible, adding layers of challenge and excitement to the gameplay.
