About Me

Hello! I am Dimitri Dagres, a 3rd-year student at Vanier College pursuing, and soon obtaining, a degree in Micromedia and Hyperpublishing. My primary areas of interest within the field of graphic design are illustration and packaging design. I have always demonstrated a strong attention to detail and persistence when working on projects. However, I am always open to new ideas and approaches to improve whatever work is being done! My collaborative spirit shines when working in groups to brainstorm new ideas on how to approach a project. I am eager to apply all the knowledge developed throughout these past years and to make an impact on the industry. The most important thing I have to offer an employer is my meticulous dedication to the craft and my readiness to learn and act!

My Work

Design Dna

Tasked with creating a compilation of images representative of my design identity. I presented it in a clean and creative format. This project aimed to showcase a visual narrative that reflects my unique design style.


Software Used: Adobe InDesign

Analog Sketch

Transforming a sketch into a graphic illustration. I recreated and colored the sketch to breathe new life into the original concept. The final illustration showcases a harmonious blend of traditional sketching and digital artistry.


Software Used: Adobe Illustrator

Poster Advertisement

For the Prix Collégial du Cinéma Québécois 2024, I designed a promotional poster that captures the essence of the cinematic event. The visual elements aim to intrigue and invite participation in the celebration of Quebecois cinema.


Software Used: Adobe Photoshop

Vintage Packaging Design

For a soap brand, I delved into the Arts and Crafts design movement to inspire the creation of packaging. The resulting design blends vintage aesthetics with a modern touch, offering a unique and visually appealing product presentation.


Software Used: Adobe Illustrator & Adobe Photoshop

Magazine Cover

For a fictitious upscale real estate magazine, I created a captivating cover design that captures the essence of luxury living. The composition and visual elements work together to entice the audience and evoke a sense of sophistication.


Software Used: Adobe InDesign

Awareness Poster

Addressing the urgent need to reduce greenhouse gas emissions, I created an awareness poster inspired by the World Wide Fund for Nature’s research. The poster visually communicates the effects of global warming on Polar Bears, aiming to inspire environmental consciousness.


Softwares Used: Adobe Photoshop & Adobe InDesign

Photo Composition

In this project, I created a realistic-looking photo composition by skillfully combining various subjects and components from separate photos. The result is a visually cohesive and compelling image that demonstrates my proficiency in photo manipulation.


Software Used: Adobe Photoshop

Public Service Announcement

Tasked with creating a PSA poster for organ donation in Alberta. I approached the design with a focus on conveying a powerful and impactful message. The resulting poster serves as a visual call to action, encouraging awareness and participation in organ donation initiatives.

Softwares Used: Adobe Illustrator & Adobe Photoshop

Amy Winehouse Album Cover

In my reimagined version of Amy Winehouse's "Back to Black" album cover, I used saturation to symbolize life's numbness and visually conveyed her struggle with drugs through the allure of drug-associated colors.


Software Used: Adobe Photoshop
